Ingredients Instruction
1 Packet MAGGI 2-Minute Noodles (Masala)
4 Tablespoons MAGGI Rich Tomato Ketchup
1 Tablespoon Oil
1 Teaspoon Cumin Seeds
2 Tomato (Finely Chopped)
1 Onion (Finely Chopped)
1 Cup Peas
1 Pinch Salt


instructions
1. Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.
2. Heat the oil and then roast the cumin seeds in it. Then,
toss in the tomatoes, the onions and cook them well. Add
the peas and the MAGGI Tomato ketchup, give it a stir,
and let them cook for a while!
3. Cook one pack of MAGGI Masala Noodles (just follow the
instructions on the pack!) and transfer it onto a dish.
4. Put the cumin-tomato-onion mix right on top of the
MAGGI noodles and you are ready to serve the easy
peasy fun!
